## **About Transcend Retail**
[Transcend Retail](https://netsoltech.com/products/digital-retail) empowers dealer groups to run smarter, more profitable operations with seamless efficiency. Its modular architecture integrates effortlessly with DMS, CRMs and third party lender systems, eliminating silos and streamlining workflows. With flexibility for multi-brand and multi-lender environments, it keeps operations smooth, while advanced analytics and dashboards deliver real-time inventory and profitability insights to drive growth without disruption. 
Seamlessly experience: 
  * **One journey, many brands:** Deliver a consistent retail flow across rooftops while honoring each OEM’s branding and policies. 
  * **Centralized programs, faster desking:** Orchestrate multi-lender, multi-OEM incentives and guardrails from the center. 
  * **Advanced analytics, actionable ops:** Cross-rooftop visibility on funnel, sales, F&I attachment and inventory health. 


Transcend Retail is more than a tool: it’s your partner in digital transformation. We help dealer groups unify multi-brand, multi-lender operations into a single, easy-to-adopt retail flow. Our API-first platform, backed by implementation support and custom development, connects websites, showroom tools, credit and F&I with analytics across rooftops. Every retail touchpoint connects into one actionable view, so you move from fragmented workflows to a unified, omnichannel model. 
‘Transcending’ retail isn’t about adding tools - it’s about rethinking how you sell cars. The result: fewer restarts, lower tool sprawl and integration cost, faster desking with guardrails and clearer visibility to [smarter dealership transitions](https://netsoltech.com/blog/smarter-dealership-operations-transitioning-without-slowing-the-sale) and customer satisfaction. 
## **Feature highlights:**
  * **Network-wide consistency:** Multi-brand, multi-OEM branding and policies with local flexibility for rooftops and regions 
  * **Multi-lender orchestration:** Route to captive/non-captive programs, tiers and approvals to speed desking at scale plus multiple configurable journeys 
  * **Omnichannel retail:** Keep the exact deal state synced across channels (no re-keying, no restarts) 
  * **Real-time, transparent payments:** Taxes, fees, incentives, lender programs and credit in one transparent calculator both customers and dealers trust 
  * **Integrated F &I, quote-to-contract: **Protections, add-ons and e-sign embedded to lift attachment and shorten cycle time 
  * Trade-in to turnkey: Instant valuations flow directly into payments, desk and contracts without swivel-chair 
  * **API-first, future-proof technology:** Plug-and-play with DMS/CRM, lenders (captive and non-captive), identity/credit, forms and OEM programs. Seamless third-party integrations for state-level compliance (taxes, incentives) 
  * **Advanced analytics and AI:** 'Next best action' recommendation; AI-enabled recommendation based on buyer behavior and buying patterns, F&I upselling as well as an AI chatbot for communications
