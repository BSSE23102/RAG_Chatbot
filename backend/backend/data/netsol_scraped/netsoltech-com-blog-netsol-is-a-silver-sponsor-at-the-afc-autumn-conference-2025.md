NETSOL is proud to announce our participation as a Silver Sponsor in this year’s AFC Autumn Conference, set to take place on November 25, 2025, in London. As the asset finance world faces seismic shifts in regulation, technology and business models, there has never been a more critical moment to engage, debate and shape the future of this industry. Our team of eight senior delegates will be on the ground for a day of rich discussion and forward looking insights. 
## **Engaging debate: The role of intermediaries in SME lending**
One of the highlights of this year’s conference is a spirited debate taking place from 5:00 pm to 5:30 pm during the event: “This House believes the future of SME lending will be direct and not via intermediaries.” 
We are especially excited to announce that our Sales Director for Europe Jason Hurwitz will be taking part in this debate, representing our vision for a more efficient, transparent and technology-driven lending model. He will argue that the [future of SME](https://finance-connect.com/sme-finance-changes-needed-for-better-outcomes-for-smes-brokers-and-lenders/) (small and medium enterprise) finance lies in direct relationships powered by digital platforms, rather than relying on traditional broker intermediaries. 
Jason’s participation underscores NETSOL’s deeply held belief: in a world where asset finance is powered by real-time data, AI-enabled risk assessment and seamless customer journeys, intermediaries’ role must evolve and direct lending is the most scalable, responsible path forward. Be sure to catch this session; it promises to be one of the most thought-provoking ones of the day.
The webinar [SME lending debate](https://netsoltech.com/insights/webinars/sme-finance-what-needs-to-change) examines this same tension in depth, exploring what structural changes are needed across the broker, lender, and SME ecosystem to unlock better outcomes.
For a sharper view of why UK asset finance brokers are under pressure to modernise their operations and what technology-driven automation actually means for their survival, the blog on the [digital imperative for brokers](https://netsoltech.com/blog/the-digital-imperative-for-brokers) covers the regulatory, competitive, and operational forces reshaping the intermediary's role.
## **NETSOL’s leadership in asset finance technology**
At NETSOL, we are deeply rooted in the [asset finance ecosystem](https://netsoltech.com/solutions/asset-finance). For decades, we’ve provided best-in-class technology solutions to global OEMs, asset finance companies, banks and other financial institutions. Here’s how we stand out: 
  * **Global footprint:** From Europe and North America to APAC and the Middle East, NETSOL has delivered in diverse markets, helping clients localize workflows, adhere to regulatory regimes and achieve rapid ROI. 
  * **Proven platform power:** Part of our unified, AI-powered [Transcend Platform](https://netsoltech.com/products/transcend), Transcend Finance supports everything from loan origination to servicing, underwriting to collections. It’s built to scale and flexible enough to adapt to new asset classes. 
  * **Cloud-native and scalable:** Our architecture is cloud-ready, enabling clients to deploy quickly while maintaining global standards of security and compliance. 
  * **Analytics and AI:** We integrate predictive risk models, credit scoring and portfolio analytics to reduce default risk and optimize collections.


NETSOL's [United Trust Bank case study](https://netsoltech.com/insights/case-studies/united-trust-bank-case-study) demonstrates this delivery capability in the UK market, a regulated UK lender that has built integrated technology infrastructure across credit origination, servicing, and reporting with NETSOL as the technology partner.
## **Why the AFC Autumn Conference matters for NETSOL**
This year’s conference comes at a pivotal moment for the asset finance sector. With regulatory pressures mounting, customer expectations evolving and technology reshaping business models, asset finance leaders must navigate change proactively. For NETSOL, attending AFC is not just about listening, it’s about joining the conversation, contributing our perspective and co-creating the future. 
This conference represents a unique opportunity for NETSOL to connect with industry leaders, regulators and innovators under one roof. With the asset finance landscape rapidly evolving, it’s critical to exchange ideas, explore emerging trends and identify practical solutions that drive efficiency and growth. 
For NETSOL, the conference is not just an event; it’s a strategic opportunity to shape the future of asset finance and reinforce our position as a trusted partner for financial institutions in Europe and worldwide.
